For Anvita

from Shreya ♥

Dear Anvita,

I keep trying to figure out how to start this, and I honestly don't think there is a clean way, so I am just going to say it: I am so genuinely grateful for you, and I am really sad you are leaving, and I don't fully know how to hold both of those feelings at the same time. So I figured I would just write it all down instead.

And since then, I have basically just lived at your place. Your apartment genuinely became one of my favorite places, not because of anything specific about it, but just because it is you. We would be on your couch doing absolutely nothing, half-watching Heartbreak High and still not finishing it, eating whatever random food (aka me eating your whole kitchen), barely studying, just talking and brainrotting and existing, and it’s always somehow the best time. And we are 100% calling and finishing it this summer!!

I also just love how naturally you include me in things. Errands, random plans, whatever you are doing, you just pull me along, like of course I’m coming, and I love that more than I probably say.

I genuinely don't think I have ever heard anyone say a single bad thing about you. Not one person. That is just you.

I joke a lot about acting like a younger sibling around you, and how I can be so unhinged and stupid, but I really just appreciate that I can be completely myself with you. I ragebait you constantly, and your shocked face every single time is one of my favorite things in the world.

I also still think about that bus ride from Pia’s, and everything you shared with me after. I carry that with so much care.

And I need you to hear this: I am always, always there. Nothing is ever too deep. Nothing is ever too much.

Next year at UCSD, I know things will change, and that makes me sad. But I also want you to go into it fully. Say yes to things. Meet people. Take up space.

Just please don't forget me when you're at UCSD (especially once you’re on Zor).

Thank you for everything. I love you so much. Go live your life fully next year. I will always be here cheering you on.

(I already know I forgot things… but maybe that’s what makes it real.)

Sincerely,
Shreya Hande
